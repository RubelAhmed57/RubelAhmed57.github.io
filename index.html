Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of South Florida (USF). I joined USF in Fall-2018 as a graduate student. I have completed my Bachelor in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET) in 2017. Before joining the doctoral program here at USF, I worked as a software engineer at Synchronous ICT (Dhaka) for about a year.

My research interest falls in the area of secure, efficient, and evolvable computing systems and my advisor is Dr. Hao Zheng. I am working on mining specification from SoC execution message flows. I want to build an efficient, general, and automatic specification mining framework that can capture the correct flow of execution of any reactive system. I am also a big fan of RISC-V Architecture.
